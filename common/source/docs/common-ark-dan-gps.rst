============
ARK DAN GPS
============

.. image:: ../../../images/arkflow/ark_dan_gps.jpg
   :target: ../_images/ark_dan_gps.jpg
   :width: 450px

Made in the USA u-blox DAN-F10N GPS and industrial magnetometer.

Specifications
==============

**Sensors**

- `u-blox DAN-F10N <https://www.u-blox.com/en/product/dan-f10n-module>`__
  
  - L1/L5/E5a/B2a bands
  - Consistently strong performance regardless of installation
  - Integrated SAW-LNA-SAW for exceptional out-of-band jamming immunity
  - u-blox F10 proprietary dual-band multipath mitigation technology

- `ST IIS2MDC Magnetometer <https://www.st.com/en/mems-and-sensors/iis2mdc.html>`__

**Connections**

- Pixhawk Standard UART/I2C Connector
- 6 Pin JST GH

**Power Requirements**

- 5V
- 25mA Average
- 44mA Max

**Other Features**

- USA Built
- NDAA Compliant
- 6 Pin Pixhawk Standard UART/I2C Cable
- LED Indicators (GPS Fix)
